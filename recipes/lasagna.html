Steps

  • first prepare the meat sauce by browning ground beef and combining it with tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and seasonings.
  • Next, make the ricotta mixture by combining ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and seasonings.
  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  • Finally, assemble the lasagna in layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese, repeating the layers until the dish is full
  • Bake the lasagna until heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
