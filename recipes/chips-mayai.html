Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  • Peel and cut potatoes into fries, then fry until golden brown.
  • Beat eggs in a bowl, add salt and chopped onions.
  • Mix the fries with the beaten eggs.
  • Pour the mixture into a pan and cook until the eggs are set.
  • Serve hot, optionally with ketchup or salad.
